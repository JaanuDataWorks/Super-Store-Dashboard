# 📊 Global Store Dashboard – Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Global Store Performance Dashboard developed using Power BI.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of business performance by tracking key metrics such as sales, profit, customer activity, order volume, and delivery efficiency.

The objective is to transform raw retail data into actionable insights that support strategic decision-making and operational improvements.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

To analyze overall store performance and identify opportunities for growth by evaluating:

- Sales and Profit Trends  
- Customer Distribution  
- Order Volume and Quantity Sold  
- Shipping Cost and Delivery Lead Time  
- State-wise Performance  

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

- Total Customers: 96  
- Total Orders: 100  
- Total Quantity Sold: 1187  
- Average Delivery Lead Time: 2.14 days  
- Total Shipping Cost: 1.59K  
- Total Sales: 82.20K  
- Total Profit: 33.27K  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- New York generates the highest sales among all states  
- High sales do not always result in high profit due to shipping costs  
- Certain states have higher delivery lead times, impacting efficiency  
- Shipping cost plays a significant role in reducing overall profitability  
- Regional performance varies significantly, indicating optimization opportunities

## ✅ Final Conclusion

The analysis highlights that while the business is generating strong sales, profitability is influenced by shipping costs and regional performance differences.

Certain states contribute significantly to revenue, while others face challenges such as higher delivery times and lower profit margins.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor performance, identify inefficiencies, and implement strategies to optimize operations, reduce costs, and improve overall business performance.
